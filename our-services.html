Creation of Innovative lessons how to integrate Lego Robots in education. Students learn how to program in Python, implementing STEM (connection between Science, Math and Programming).

Multiculturality, diversity, integration - equal opportunities for everyone (2019-1-RO01-KA229-063448_6)

These are the most important challenges for today’s Europe. In a more and more diverse and multicultural society people face with a dual situation: to be able for a deeply integration or to preserve their cultural/ethnic identity. This project will help our students to come closer to different cultures and get familiar to different traditions and different ways of life.

Roboloco (2020-1-NO01-KA201-076502)

Creating educational video game in order to improve computational thinking at high school students. Through entertainment, they will learn programming concepts, better understanding and implementing programming languages in general. This is mobile game ( race with robots ), students compete with each other and the one with best code wins the race.

Hiclass (2020-1-IT02-KA226-SCH-095699)

Project aim is to find out new educational online practices, which became necessary in schools, especially after pandemics . Results are integrated digital didactics as a methodology that is not of an emergency nature but structured in the educational organisational fabric of the schools in the partnership . The new techniques involve blended learning – collaboration between “online students” and students in the school.
