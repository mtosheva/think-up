Read About Us

Think Up Digital is NGO founded in 2022, after realizing that Erasmus courses offered lack of digital topics, which are more than needed for every profile, especially in education sector.

The main goal of the Association TINK UP DIGITAL is the advocacy of initiatives related to improving the digital literacy of the population, raising public awareness of the importance of quality computer skills. The association is taking initiatives to improve digital skills, basic and advanced skills in coding, web design, online learning and similar competences, which already became obligatory especially during pandemic and after. Need of this skills was approved: only those who worked online continued to work even better, while others lost their jobs.

The association has capacity to work on STEM projects, as well as projects dealing with climate change, green agenda, youth sector, democratic involvement of youth, building European values.

Although the NGO is new, our team has a lot of experience in projects , 10 years in Erasmus projectrs and 20 years in International projects.
