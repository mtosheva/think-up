Computational Thinking and Design in Scratch

Scratch training (Program for students)


5-Day Student Training:

Day 1 - Introduction to Computational Thinking and Coding
Introduction to Computational Thinking (CT) concepts Overview of Scratch programming language Hands-on activities: Basic Scratch programming exercises


Day 2 - Exploring Scratch Projects
Review of Scratch projects and their functionalities
Interactive session: Analyzing and modifying existing Scratch projects Guided project: Creating a simple game or animation in Scratch


Day 3 - Advanced Scratch Programming
Advanced Scratch programming techniques Project-based learning: Collaborative Scratch project development Peer review and feedback session


Day 4 - Integrating CT into Projects
Applying Computational Thinking in project design Group project: Developing interactive projects using CT principles Presentation of group projects


Day 5 - Showcase and Reflection
Presentation of individual and group projects to peers Reflection on learning outcomes and application of CT skills Closing ceremony and certificate distribution



Scratch training (Program for teachers)


5-Day Teacher Training:

Day 1 - Introduction to Computational Thinking and Coding
Understanding the importance of Computational Thinking (CT) in education Overview of coding concepts and their relevance to the curriculum Exploring resources for integrating coding into various subjects


Day 2 - Curriculum Integration Strategies
Strategies for integrating coding activities into existing curriculum Lesson planning with coding: Designing engaging activities aligned with learning objectives Hands-on session: Creating sample lesson plans incorporating coding


Day 3 - Effective Coding Pedagogy
Best practices for teaching coding to students of different ages and abilities Creating a supportive learning environment for coding activities Collaborative workshop: Sharing and discussing effective teaching methods


Day 4 - Assessment and Feedback
Developing assessment strategies for coding activities Providing constructive feedback to students on coding projects Peer review and evaluation of coding lesson plans


Day 5 - Implementation and Sustainability
Planning for sustainable integration of coding into the curriculum Identifying resources and support networks for ongoing professional development Action planning: Setting goals for implementing coding initiatives in schools


These training outlines aim to provide comprehensive instruction and hands-on experience for both students and teachers, equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to effectively integrate Computational Thinking and coding into their learning and teaching practices.
