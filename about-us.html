Read About Us

Think Up Digital was established with the recognition that Erasmus courses often lack digital topics, crucial for every profession, particularly in the education sector. Our association is dedicated to advocating for initiatives that enhance digital literacy and promote the significance of quality computer skills. Furthermore, we are dedicated to any kind of project, with a focus on equality, wellbeing, saving our planet, inclusion, with participants from pre-school to senior age.

We prioritize initiatives aimed at improving digital skills, ranging from basic to advanced competencies in IT skills, coding, web design, online learning, and related areas, which have become increasingly essential, especially in the wake of the pandemic. The necessity for these skills has been underscored by the fact that individuals who could work online continued to thrive, while others faced challenges or lost their jobs.

With a foundation in STEM projects and a commitment to addressing issues such as climate change, the green agenda, youth engagement, and the promotion of European values, Think Up Digital is poised to make a meaningful impact. Despite being formally established in 2022, our team boasts extensive experience spanning 12 years in Erasmus projects and 20 years in international collaborations, also outside Europe.

The idea for our association

he idea of the founder came out naturally after the successful accomplishment of several international projects in USA and Europe, working as a teacher in a public high school. The founder Sofija Tosheva was mentioned over 100 times in scientific articles for her contribution with her master these: “Adaptive E-learning System in Education”, especially after pandemics. She started working on the topic in 2010, resulting in students achieving more results when they have online tools for adaptive learning styles. Further she worked with university professors and a few articles were produced and published at International Conferences for innovative blended learning.

More about us and our country

We are located in Strumica, N Macedonia, but we can also organize mobilities in Skopje, the capital. We have experience in implementation of Erasmus projects, organizing mobilities starting from 3 to 35 participants. Opposite of other associations, we do not offer European big cities, because these are places that you can eventually visit sometime. But this is your chance to meet very hospitable people and taste affordable high-quality local food and wine.

Furthermore, we aim to highlight the beauty and culture of North Macedonia, particularly the charming sunny town of Strumica, enticing visitors with our traditional Balkan hospitality.
